# Green's Theorem

%%CW_CARD_START uid:Green%%
Green's Theorem relates a line integral around a simple closed curve to a double integral over the plane region bounded by the curve.

Mathematically: ∮(L dx + M dy) = ∬(∂M/∂x - ∂L/∂y) dA

It is a special case of the more general Stokes' Theorem.
%%CW_CARD_END uid:Green%%

## Applications

- Computing area from a boundary integral
- Fluid circulation and flux in two dimensions
- Foundation for higher-dimensional integration theorems
